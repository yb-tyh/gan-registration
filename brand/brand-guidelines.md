# Brand Identity Guidelines: Morah Bati's Gan

## 1. Brand Essence

- **Brand Name:** Morah Bati's Gan
- **Tagline:** Where little minds grow with joy!
- **Vibe:** Nurturing, creative, and educational.
- **Mission:** A Jewish, play-based learning environment for children ages 2–3.
- **Visual Style:** High-contrast foundational colors anchored by organic, earthy tones.

## 2. Primary Logo

File: `brand/logo.png`

The logo consists of a winding **Chocolate Brown** tree trunk with a randomized canopy of primary-colored building blocks and a single **Sapphire Blue** book.

- **Primary Text:** "Morah Bati's Gan" in **Deep Forest Green**.
- **Tagline:** "Where little minds grow with joy!" in **Chocolate Brown**.
- **Usage:** Place on a white or very light cream background. Ensure a "clear zone" around the logo equal to the height of the letter "M" in the brand name.

## 4. Typography

- **Primary Heading Font:** **Zilla Slab** or **Arvo** (Bold) — Slab serif, academic yet friendly. Use for page titles.
- **Secondary/Body Font:** **Quicksand** (Rounded Sans-Serif) — Legible and soft. Use for paragraphs, forms, body copy.
- **Fallback Fonts:** Serif (headings), Sans-Serif (body).
- **Google Fonts import:** `Zilla+Slab:wght@700&family=Quicksand:wght@400;600`

## 5. UI/UX Components

- **Buttons:** `border-radius: 20px`, Sapphire Blue (`#0D47A1`) background, white text.
- **Containers/Cards:** Light-colored cards with subtle box-shadow — evoke "building blocks" of information.
- **Icons:** Simple, chunky, flat vector icons. Avoid thin or clinical lines.
- **Inputs:** Rounded corners, warm border colors, no harsh lines.

## 6. Brand Don'ts

- Do **not** use neon or electric colors. Stick to foundational tones.
- Do **not** use thin, hard-to-read script fonts.
- Do **not** remove the exclamation mark from the tagline — it is part of the brand's joyful energy.
- Do **not** place the logo on dark or busy backgrounds.
